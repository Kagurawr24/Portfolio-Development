About Me



Hi! I am Shi Qi, studying Diploma in Digital Design and Development at Republic Polytechnic. I’m passionate about UI design in web development, striving to blend functionality with visual appeal. My interest in web design began with an inspirational poster featuring Jeffrey Zeldman’s thoughts, motivating me to explore this creative field further. I focus on simplicity and consistency, always aligning with the client’s vision. I primarily use Visual Studio Code to integrate my design skills with coding, creating intuitive, user-friendly interfaces.

Outside of design, I enjoy solo travel or trips with close friends to enjoy scenic night landscapes. I also practiced wushu for nearly nine years and served as vice-captain in both primary and secondary school, which instilled in me discipline and focus—qualities I apply in my design work.

In UI/UX design, I aim to create seamless, engaging interfaces and look forward to continually refining my skills and crafting memorable websites.
